Curiosities about Angola

Angola is a sub-Saharan African country

1— Language

Although many people think we speak French, Angolan people actually speak Portuguese. We are one of the 5 portuguese African colonies. Our people have indignes languages such as: Kimbundu, Umbundu, Nganguela.

2— Fauna and Flora

We have unique fauna and flora. In fact we have the “Palanca Negra Gigante”, a giant antelope that can only be found in Angola. In addition, we have a desert flower discovered by the Austrian Botanist Friedrich Welwistch, name “welwistchia mirabilis” (for this one, we have to share credits with Namibia :|). Mayombe is our biggest rainforest, rich in wood.

3— Rhythms (Music and Dance)

Semba, kuduro, Kizomba, tarraxinha are the main rhythms that heat up even more our land. Go to Angola and brush up your dance moves ;)

4— Angolan beauties

Leila Lopes, an angolan woman was miss Universe 2011. Maria Borges and Sharam Diniz were Victoria Secret Models. Other beautiful Angolan women worth mentioning are: Lesliana Pereira, Micaela Reis, Vaumara Rebelo and Amilna Estevao. We have beautiful men too like: Freddy Costa and Carlos Nascimento.

5— Richness

the country is rich in oil and diamonds, however it does not reflect on the lives of all the population. Also in: gas natural, iron, copper and gold, lead, wood, coffee...

6— Touristic sites

Places worth visiting: kalandula falls in Malanje, Fortress Sao Miguel, Quissama National Park, Mussulo Island, Museum of Natural History, National Anthropology Museum,etc.

7— Sports

Favourite sport is basketball, 11 times African champions. Although handball, football, hockey and volleyball are also very popular.

8— Entertainment

Started producing soap opera and movies recently; the soap opera Windeck was nominated for a grammy.

9— The richest woman

One of the richest woman in Africa is the Angolan Isabel dos Santos

10— The Angolan queen

Nzinga Mbandi was an Angolan warrior queen in XVII century War and Independence

11— War and Independence

Angola gained its independence from the Portuguese on the 11th November 1975, afterwards had a civil war between the political parties MPLA and UNITA that ended in 2002.

12— Literature

A few Famous writers: Agostinho Neto (former and first President), Pepetela, Uanhenga Xitu, also known as Mendes de Carvalho

Learn with me!

Our Language is portuguese but we decided to give it a little tropical flavour, so we mixed kimbundu, umbundu, kikongo, nguanguela and others. You may find strange but as any other nation we have our own way to say things.

Please find some examples below. I will put first the english, then portuguese, flower by portuguese with an angolan flavour

breakfast = pequeno almoço = matabicho

sandwich = sandes/sanduíche = magoga*

* it is a specific sandwich made with fried chicken and mayo

beer = cerveja = birra

drunk = bêbado = boiado

hungry = esfomeado = fobado

money = dinheiro = kumbu

friend = amigo/amiga = kamba

woman = mulher = mboa

party = festa = boda

hug = abraço = kandandu

very/much = muito = bué

to leave = ir embora = bazar

home/house = casa = biva /Kubico

anyone older than you (sign of respect) = mais velho = kota

anyone younger than you = mais novo = ndengue/puto
